Mandazi Recipe

Dry Ingredients

  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp instant yeast
  • 5tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp melted butter/margarine
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Add the wet ingredients into a separate bowl and mix well
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ones and knead into a dough
  4. Cover with a damp cloth and set aside to rise for 1 hr or until double in size
  5. roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface. Roll it to a height of 1 cm then cut. You can cut square shapes or even use the bottom of a glass to cut round shapes.
  6. Fry. Enjoy
